Genesis Manifesto: Agriculture Edition

Food security and climate resilience require collaboration, but not surveillance. Sovereign Mohawk Agriculture Global exists to prove that farms can co-train frontier models while retaining full control over their own operational and biological data.

We reject extractive data pipelines. We champion sovereign participation, cryptographic accountability, and policy-governed machine learning that respects local laws, community governance, and farmer rights.

Every round must be attested. Every transfer must be auditable. Every farm must remain sovereign.
