# GENESIS_MANIFESTO

Sovereign Mohawk Agriculture Global is a commitment to farmer-first AI systems.

## Principles

- Sovereignty first: no raw farm data leaves local custody.
- Collaboration without centralization: global model quality from local intelligence.
- Verifiable trust: attestation, proofs, and policy signatures are mandatory.
- Compliance by design: governance and data controls are built into the runtime.
- Resilience for all: from smallholder deployments to industrial-scale co-ops.
